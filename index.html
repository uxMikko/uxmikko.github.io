About Me

Senior UX/product designer with 5+ years across B2B SaaS, public health infrastructure, and pharma. I work best on complex products for technical users — developers, data scientists, scientists, engineers. People who know exactly what they need and notice when a tool disrespects that.

My approach starts with discovery. Design is the output, not the starting point. I run research, synthesise it into product direction, and navigate multi-stakeholder environments with conflicting priorities, regulatory constraints, and engineering limitations.
