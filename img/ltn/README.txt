Images for the LTN case study.

Drop your exported Figma screenshots here.

To use an image, replace the .img-placeholder div in ltn/index.html with:
  <img src="../img/ltn/your-image.png" alt="Description" style="width:100%; border-radius:16px;">

Recommended filenames:
  hero.png        — sign-in screen or portal overview (2400x1040px ideal)
  discovery.png   — manual process mapping / research notes
  wizard.png      — step-by-step registration flow screens
  dashboard.png   — agency staff review dashboard
