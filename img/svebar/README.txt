Images for the svebar case study.

Drop your exported Figma screenshots here.

To use an image, replace the .img-placeholder div in svebar/index.html with:
  <img src="../img/svebar/your-image.png" alt="Description" style="width:100%; border-radius:16px;">

Recommended filenames:
  hero.png        — wide header / hero screen (2400×1040px ideal)
  discovery.png   — research synthesis / workshop photo
  wireframes.png  — lo-fi or mid-fi screens
  hifi.png        — final hi-fidelity screens
